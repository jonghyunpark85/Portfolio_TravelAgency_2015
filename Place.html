CHRISTMAS MARKETS TIME

Social 100 09 January 2015 17 replys

This annual market aims to nurture independent Australian design and has all areas covered for your Christmas shopping, with artists showcasing unique work, including jewellery, fashion, furniture, homewares as well as items from the Whitehouse Institute of Design.

Designers are hand chosen to ensure a balance of range and price to suit all tastes and budgets. There will also be food stalls keeping the shopping munchies at bay and giving you the fuel to find that last perfect gift.
